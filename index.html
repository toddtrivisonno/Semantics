Guatemala

Guatemala is easily overlooked, south of it's much larger neighbor, Mexico, and sandwiched together with many other smaller countries. And though the news will regularly tell you it's a dangerous place to be avoided, a little time there reveals it's full of wonderful, accomodating people and culture.

Reconnecting in Guatemala

After three years avoiding each other, literally moving to different countries to do so, we reconnected in the beautiful country of Guatemala.

Germany

Germany became our first home together. After our time in Guatemala together we decided to tie the knot and Megan moved with me to Germany. Looking back, we must have seemed crazy...my boss did suspect I had mail-ordered a bride...but we knew what we were getting ourselves into.

Beers and Buddys

Talking about Germany wouldn't be complete without talking about the great friends we made while sampling Germany's finest beer halls.

Italy

It total we lived in Italy for just over four years. We loved Germany, Guatemala, and the many places we were able to visit. But Italy was a place that we could comfortably call home.

Adding a life. Life-changing Addy.
